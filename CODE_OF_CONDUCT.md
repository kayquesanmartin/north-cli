# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
north community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and any
conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional
setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
maintainer through a [GitHub private report](https://github.com/kayquesanmartin/north-cli/security/advisories/new)
or by opening a confidential channel with the repository owner. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
